Tourism

Sunnydale Zoo

Here at the Sunnydale zoo, you can see over 150 species of animals. Teenagers can help out in the summer or apply for an internship. There are family discounts and season passes. In the summer, there are many events and camps for children to attend and learn about the animals or pursuing a career in zoology. Of course, tickets are available daily. In the winter, they have indoor caves to see the winter animals and still stay warm such as polar bears and penguins. Many people come to the Sunnydale zoo to find some of the most exotic animals. With over 150 species of animals in our zoo.

Museum

Our city also has a museum. The building dates back to the War of 1812. One can tour the museum Monday through Friday form 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Saturdays they are open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. The museum contains many artifacts and interesting facts about the history of Pennsylvania and our city.
