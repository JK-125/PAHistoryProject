The Shining City

Sunnydale is a suburb just outside of Springfield PA. The population is 75,000 as of 2019's statistics. It has one larger public High School District and one private school to choose from. There is a large shopping mall with several additional specialty plazas's and grocery stores. Crime statistics are 30% lower than the national average. We are ranked the 3rd safest city in the United States.
